BigNumExpressionGrammar Grammar

Expr → Term (('+' | '-') Term)*
Term → Factor (('*' | '/') Factor)*
Factor → ('-' Factor) | ('+' Factor) | Number | '(' Expr ')'
Number → BigFloatLiteral | BigRationalLiteral | BigIntLiteral
ExponentPart → token([eE] ('+' | '-')? digit+)
BigFloatLiteral → token(digit+ '.' digit+ ExponentPart? | digit+ ExponentPart)
BigRationalLiteral → token('[' (digit+ ('/' digit+)?) ']')
BigIntLiteral → token(digit+)
Spaces → space+
