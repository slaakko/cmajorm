DeclarationGrammar Grammar

Declarations → (Declaration)*
Declaration → NamespaceDefinition | LinkageSpecification | FunctionDefinition | SpecialMemberFunctionDefinition | TemplateDeclaration | ExplicitInstantiation | ExplicitSpecialization | ClassDeclaration | EnumDeclaration | BlockDeclaration
NamespaceDefinition → NamedNamespaceDefinition | UnnamedNamespaceDefinition
NamedNamespaceDefinition → keyword("namespace") Identifier '{' Declarations '}'!
UnnamedNamespaceDefinition → keyword("namespace") '{' Declarations '}'!
BlockDeclaration → SimpleDeclaration | AliasDeclaration | UsingDirective | UsingDeclaration | StaticAssertDeclaration | OpaqueEnumDeclaration | TypedefDeclaration | ForwardClassDeclaration
SimpleDeclaration → (DeclSpecifiers TypeExpr InitDeclarator ';')
DeclSpecifiers → (DeclSpecifier)*
DeclSpecifier → StorageClassSpecifier | FunctionSpecifier | keyword("friend") | keyword("constexpr") | keyword("__declspec") ('(' keyword("thread") ')' | '(' keyword("dllimport") ')' | '(' keyword("dllexport") ')') | keyword("__thread")
StorageClassSpecifier → keyword("register") | keyword("static") | keyword("thread_local") | keyword("extern") | keyword("mutable")
FunctionSpecifier → keyword("inline") | keyword("virtual") | keyword("explicit")
AliasDeclaration → (keyword("using") Identifier '=' TypeExpr! ';'!)
UsingDirective → (keyword("using") keyword("namespace") QualifiedId! ';'!)
UsingDeclaration → (keyword("using") QualifiedId ';')
StaticAssertDeclaration → (keyword("static_assert") '(' ConstantExpression ',' StringLiteral ')' ';')
TypedefDeclaration → (keyword("typedef") TypeExpr! Declarator! ';'!)
LinkageSpecification → keyword("extern") StringLiteral ('{' (Declaration)* '}'! | Declaration)
