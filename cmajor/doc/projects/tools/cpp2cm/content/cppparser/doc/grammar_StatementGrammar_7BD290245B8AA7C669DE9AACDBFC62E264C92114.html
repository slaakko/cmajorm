StatementGrammar Grammar

Statement → LabeledStatement | ExpressionStatement | CompoundStatement | SelectionStatement | IterationStatement | JumpStatement | DeclarationStatement | TryBlock
LabeledStatement → (Identifier (':' − "::") Statement) | (keyword("case") ConstantExpression! ':'! Statement!) | (keyword("default") ':'! Statement!)
ExpressionStatement → (Expression? ';')
CompoundStatement → '{' (Statement)* '}'!
SelectionStatement → (keyword("if") '('! Condition! ')'! Statement! (keyword("else") Statement!)?) | (keyword("switch") '('! Condition! ')'! Statement!)
Condition → TypeExpr Declarator ('=' InitializerClause | BracedInitializerList) | Expression
IterationStatement → (keyword("while") '('! Condition! ')'! Statement!) | (keyword("do") Statement! keyword("while")! '('! Expression! ')'! ';'!) | (keyword("for") '(' ForRangeDeclaration ':' ForRangeInitializer! ')'! Statement!) | (keyword("for") '(' ForInitialization Condition? ';' Expression? ')'! Statement!)
ForInitialization → ExpressionStatement | SimpleDeclaration
ForRangeDeclaration → (TypeExpr Declarator)
ForRangeInitializer → Expression | BracedInitializerList
JumpStatement → (keyword("break") ';'!) | (keyword("continue") ';'!) | (keyword("return") Expression? ';'!) | (keyword("return") BracedInitializerList ';'!) | (keyword("goto") Identifier! ';'!)
DeclarationStatement → BlockDeclaration
TryBlock → keyword("try") (CompoundStatement)! HandlerSequence!
HandlerSequence → (Handler)+
Handler → (keyword("catch") '('! ExceptionDeclaration! ')'! CompoundStatement!)
ExceptionDeclaration → (TypeExpr Declarator?) | "..."
