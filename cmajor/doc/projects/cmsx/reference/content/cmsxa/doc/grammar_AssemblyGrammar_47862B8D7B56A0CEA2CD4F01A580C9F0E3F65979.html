AssemblyGrammar Grammar

DecimalConstant → token(digit+)
HexConstant → token('#' (hexdigit+))
CharacterConstant → '\'' [^\n'] '\''
StringConstant → '\"' ([^\"\n]*) '\"'
Constant → DecimalConstant | HexConstant | CharacterConstant | StringConstant
LocalSymbol → token('@' digit+)
Symbol → token((idstart | ':' | '_') (idcont | ':' | '_')*)
At → '@'
PrimaryExpression → token(LocalSymbol | Constant | Symbol | At | ('(' Expression ')') | (UnaryOperator PrimaryExpression))
UnaryOperator → '+' | '-' | '~' | '$' | '&'
Term → token(PrimaryExpression (StrongOperator PrimaryExpression)*)
StrongOperator → '*' | '/' | "//" | '%' | "<<" | ">>" | '&'
Expression → token(Term (WeakOperator Term)*)
WeakOperator → '+' | '-' | '|' | '^'
Instruction → (Label SNL OpCode Operands)
InstructionLine → Instruction (S? ';' Instruction)* Comment? NL
Label → LocalSymbol | Symbol | empty
OpCode → token((letter | digit)+)
Operands → token(S Expression (',' Expression)* | S? empty)
S → token([ \t]+)
SNL → token(S (NL S)? | empty NL S)
NL → token("\r\n" | '\n' | '\r')
Comment → S? (anychar − (idstart | ':' | '_' | '@' | ';' | '.' | NL)) (anychar − NL)*
CommentLine → Comment NL
EmptyLine → S? NL
ModeLine → token(S? '.' [cC] [oO] [dD] [eE]) S? NL | token(S? '.' [dD] [aA] [tT] [aA]) S? NL
AssemblyLine → CommentLine | EmptyLine | ModeLine | InstructionLine
AssemblyFile → AssemblyLine*
