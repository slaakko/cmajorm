ExprGrammar Grammar

Expression → FormatExpression
WeakOperator → '+' | '-' | '|' | '^'
StrongOperator → '*' | '/' | '%' | "<<" | ">>" | '&'
UnaryOperator → '+' | '-' | '~' | '*'
Format → (('b' | '1') | ('w' | '2') | ('t' | '4') | ('o' | '8') | 'c' | 's') ('s' | 'x')?
FormatExpression → BinaryExpression ('.' Format)?
BinaryExpression → Term (WeakOperator Term)*
Term → UnaryExpression (StrongOperator UnaryExpression)*
UnaryExpression → ('(' Expression ')') | UnaryOperator UnaryExpression | PrimaryExpression
PrimaryExpression → Segment | RegNumber | DecNumber | HexNumber | Symbol
Segment → "$t" | "$d" | "$p" | "$s" | "$k"
RegNumber → ('$' byte)
DecNumber → ulong
HexNumber → ('#' hex)
Symbol → identifier
