RegularExpressionGrammar Grammar

RegularExpression → AlternativeExpression
AlternativeExpression → SequenceExpression ('|' SequenceExpression)*
SequenceExpression → PostfixExpression (PostfixExpression)*
PostfixExpression → PrimaryExpression ('*' | '+' | '?')?
PrimaryExpression → Char | '.' | CharClass | ('(' AlternativeExpression ')') | "\\d" | "\\D" | "\\s" | "\\S" | "\\w" | "\\W" | "\\l" | "\\L" | "\\u" | "\\U" | escape
Char → (anychar − [()\\[*+?|.])
CharClass → ('[' ('^')? (Class | CharRange)* ']'!)
Class → "[:alnum:]" | "[:alpha:]" | "[:blank:]" | "[:cntrl:]" | "[:digit:]" | "[:graph:]" | "[:lower:]" | "[:print:]" | "[:punct:]" | "[:space:]" | "[:upper:]" | "[:word:]" | "[:w:]" | "[:xdigit:]"
CharRange → CharClassChar ('-' CharClassChar | empty)
CharClassChar → [^\\]] | escape
