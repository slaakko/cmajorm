HTTP Date Grammar

http‑date → rfc‑1123‑date | rfc‑850‑date | asc‑time‑date
rfc‑1123‑date → wkday , sp date1 sp time sp GMT
rfc‑850‑date → weekday , sp date2 sp time sp GMT
asc‑time‑date → wkday sp date3 sp time sp year
date1 → day sp month sp year
date2 → day - month - year2
date3 → month sp (day | day1)
year → digit digit digit digit
year2 → digit digit
day → digit digit
day1 → sp digit
wkday → Mon | Tue | Wed | Thu | Fri | Sat | Sun
weekday → Monday | Tuesday | Wednesday | Thursday | Friday | Saturday | Sunday
month → Jan | Feb | Mar | Apr | May | Jun | Jul | Aug | Sep | Oct | Nov | Dec
time → hour : min : sec
hour → digit digit
min → digit digit
sec → digit digit
delta‑seconds → digit+
sp →
digit → [0-9]
