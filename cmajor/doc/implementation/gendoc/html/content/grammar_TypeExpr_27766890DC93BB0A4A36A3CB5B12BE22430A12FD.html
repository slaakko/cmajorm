TypeExpr Grammar

Rules

TypeExpr → PrefixTypeExpr
PrefixTypeExpr → (keyword("const") PostfixTypeExpr) | PostfixTypeExpr
PostfixTypeExpr → PrimaryTypeExpr ('.' Identifier! | '*' | "&&" | '&' | ('[' Expression? ']'!))*
PrimaryTypeExpr → BasicType | TemplateId | Identifier
