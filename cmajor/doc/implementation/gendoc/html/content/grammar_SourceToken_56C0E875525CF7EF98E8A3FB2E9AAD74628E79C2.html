SourceToken Grammar

Rules

SourceTokens → SourceToken* [^\r\n]* newline?
SourceToken → PPKeyword | Keyword | QualifiedId | (FloatingLiteral | IntegerLiteral) | CharLiteral | StringLiteral | Spaces | LineComment | block_comment | newline | Other
Spaces → [\t ]+
LineComment → "//"
Other → [^\r\n]
