Literal Grammar

Rules

Literal → BooleanLiteral | FloatingLiteral | IntegerLiteral | CharLiteral | StringLiteral | NullLiteral | ArrayLiteral | StructuredLiteral
BooleanLiteral → keyword("true") | keyword("false")
FloatingLiteral → token(FloatingLiteralValue ([fF] | empty))
FloatingLiteralValue → token(FractionalFloatingLiteral | ExponentFloatingLiteral)
FractionalFloatingLiteral → token(DecDigitSequence? '.' DecDigitSequence ExponentPart?) | token(DecDigitSequence '.')
ExponentFloatingLiteral → token(DecDigitSequence ExponentPart)
ExponentPart → token([eE] Sign? DecDigitSequence)
IntegerLiteral → token(IntegerLiteralValue ([uU] | empty))
IntegerLiteralValue → token(HexIntegerLiteral | DecIntegerLiteral)
HexIntegerLiteral → token(("0x" | "0X") HexDigitSequence)
DecIntegerLiteral → DecDigitSequence
CharLiteral → token('\'' (([^\'\\\r\n]+) | CharEscape) '\''!) | 'w' token('\'' (([^\'\\\r\n]+) | CharEscape) '\''!) | 'u' token('\'' (([^\'\\\r\n]+) | CharEscape) '\''!)
StringLiteral → '@' token('\"' ([^\"]*) '\"') | 'w' '@' token('\"' ([^\"]*) '\"') | 'u' '@' token('\"' ([^\"]*) '\"') | token('\"' (([^\"\\\r\n]+) | CharEscape)* '\"'!) | 'w' token('\"' (([^\"\\\r\n]+) | CharEscape)* '\"'!) | 'u' token('\"' (([^\"\\\r\n]+) | CharEscape)* '\"'!)
NullLiteral → keyword("null")
ArrayLiteral → '[' (Expression % ',')? ']'!
StructuredLiteral → '{' (Expression % ',')? '}'!
CharEscape → token('\\' ([xX] HexDigitSequence | [dD] DecDigitSequence | OctalDigitSequence | 'u' HexDigit4 | 'U' HexDigit8 | 'a' | 'b' | 'f' | 'n' | 'r' | 't' | 'v' | anychar))
DecDigitSequence → token(digit+)
HexDigitSequence → token(hexdigit+)
HexDigit4 → token(hexdigit hexdigit hexdigit hexdigit)
HexDigit8 → token(hexdigit hexdigit hexdigit hexdigit hexdigit hexdigit hexdigit hexdigit)
OctalDigitSequence → token([0-7])+
Sign → '+' | '-'
